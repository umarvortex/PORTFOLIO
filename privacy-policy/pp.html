Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal information on my portfolio website. By using this site, you agree to this policy.

Key Point: I don't sell your data. All information is handled with care and used solely for the purposes described below.

1. Information I Collect

On my portfolio site, I may collect:

  • Contact Information: When you use my contact form, I collect your name, email address, and message content.
  • Usage Data: Through analytics tools, I collect anonymous data about pages visited, duration, and referral sources.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance site functionality.
  • Blog Comments: If you comment on blog posts, I collect your name, email, and comment content.

2. How I Use Your Data

Your information helps me to:

  • Respond to your inquiries through the contact form
  • Improve my website based on usage analytics
  • Maintain blog discussions (if applicable)
  • Showcase my work to potential clients
  • Prevent spam and maintain security

3. Third-Party Services

I use these trusted services:

  • Website Hosting: My portfolio is hosted on [ GitHub ]
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for traffic analysis (anonymized data)
  • Form Processing: [ Formspree ] for contact form submissions

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

4. Cookies & Tracking

This site uses:

  • Essential cookies for basic functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand visitor behavior
  • Session cookies for features like contact forms

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Data Protection

I implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfers
  • Regular software updates
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Secure form processing

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Ask for corrections or deletions
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Policy Updates

I may update this policy as my portfolio evolves. Significant changes will be noted below:
