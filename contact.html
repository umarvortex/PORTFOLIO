Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about working with me

What is your typical process for new projects?

My process typically starts with a discovery phase where we discuss your goals, target audience, and requirements. After gathering all necessary information, I create a project plan, wireframes, and design mockups for approval. Once the designs are finalized, I move on to development, testing, and deployment.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex applications can take 2-3 months or more. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both fixed-price and hourly rate options depending on the project. For most client projects, I prefer a fixed-price approach with clear deliverables and payment milestones. This ensures transparency and helps you budget accordingly. Contact me for a custom quote based on your project requirements.

Do you offer maintenance services after project completion?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure your website or application continues to run smoothly. This includes regular updates, security patches, content updates, and technical support. I offer monthly maintenance packages or hourly support as needed.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in frontend technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, and Vue.js. For backend development, I work with Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and SQL databases. I'm also experienced with content management systems like WordPress and e-commerce platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce.

How do we communicate during the project?

I maintain regular communication through your preferred channels, such as email, Slack, or scheduled video calls. I provide weekly progress updates and milestone reviews to ensure the project stays on track and meets your expectations.
