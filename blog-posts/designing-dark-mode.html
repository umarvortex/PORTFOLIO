Designing for Dark Mode

Dark mode has evolved from a niche preference to a mainstream design feature. With major operating systems, browsers, and applications now offering dark themes, users increasingly expect this option in the digital products they use. Beyond aesthetic appeal, dark mode offers practical benefits like reduced eye strain in low-light environments, potential battery savings on OLED displays, and accessibility advantages for certain users.

This guide explores best practices and key considerations when implementing dark mode in your web and mobile applications, helping you create an effective and beautiful dark theme that enhances the user experience.
